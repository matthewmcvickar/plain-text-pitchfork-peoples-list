Plain-text Pitchfork People’s List

Work out your People’s List in plain text before dragging-and-dropping.

By Matthew McVickar.

  1. Build your People’s List with reckless excitement and nostalgia.
  2. When you’ve got a good list, select the entire webpage (⌘+A on Mac, Ctrl+A on Windows) and copy. The fully selected webpage should look like this.
  3. Paste it in the textbox below and click the GO button.
  4. Your album list will be automatically generated. Reorder it with the ease of copy and paste commands (preferably in a text editor—I’m not auto-saving anything here).
  5. When you're done, use it as a reference to order your People’s List.

If it doesn’t work, let me know!
